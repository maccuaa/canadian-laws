# Bill C-19 — Canada Groceries and Essentials Benefit Act

## Summary

This enactment amends thein order to increase the maximum annual Goods and Services Tax/Harmonized Sales Tax credit (GSTC) amounts by 50% for the 2025-2026 benefit year. It also amends that Act to increase the maximum annual GSTC amounts by 25% as of the 2026-2027 benefit year for a period of five years. Income Tax Act
