# Bill C-217 — An Act to amend the Canada Student Financial Assistance Act and the  Income Tax Act

## Summary

This enactment provides for financial assistance for Canadians with disabilities to improve their access to post-secondary education.

## Alternative Title

### Section 1
*Alternative title*

This Act may be cited as the Post-Secondary Education Financial Assistance for Persons with Disabilities Act .
