# Bill S-226 — Jury Duty Appreciation Week Act

## Summary

This enactment designates the second week of May in each and every year as “Jury Duty Appreciation Week”.

## Jury Duty Appreciation Week

### Section 2
*Jury Duty Appreciation Week*

Throughout Canada, in each and every year, the second week of May shall be known as “Jury Duty Appreciation Week”.
