# Bill C-203 — An Act respecting Ukrainian Heritage Month

## Summary

This enactment designates the month of September as “Ukrainian Heritage Month”.

## Short Title

### Section 1
*Short title*

This Act may be cited as the Ukrainian Heritage Month Act .

## Ukrainian Heritage Month

### Section 2
*Ukrainian Heritage Month*

Throughout Canada, in each year, the month of September is to be known as “Ukrainian Heritage Month”.
