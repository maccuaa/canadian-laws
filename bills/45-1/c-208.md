# Bill C-208 — An Act to recognize a national livestock brand as a symbol of Canada and of western and frontier heritage

## Summary

This enactment recognizes and declares a livestock brand as the national livestock brand of Canada.

## Short Title

### Section 1
*Short title*

This Act may be cited as the National Livestock Brand of Canada Act .

## National Livestock Brand

### Section 2
*National livestock brand*

The livestock brand illustrated in the schedule is hereby recognized and declared to be the national livestock brand of Canada.
