# Bill C-272 — An Act to amend the Controlled Drugs and Substances Act (supervised drug consumption sites)

## Summary

This enactment amends theto provide that regulations made and exemptions granted under that Act must not have the effect of allowing a site providing services in relation to the consumption of certain substances to be located within 500 metres of an elementary or secondary school, a daycare centre or a playground.
