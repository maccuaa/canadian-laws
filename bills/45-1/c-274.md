# Bill C-274 — An Act to amend the Citizenship Act and the Immigration and Refugee Protection Act

## Summary

This enactment amends theto provide a path to citizenship for certain persons who were not citizens when they transitioned out of the care of a child welfare agency or foster parent. It also amends theto provide that a removal order cannot be enforced against such persons who are applying for citizenship until a final decision is made on their application.
