# Bill C-209 — An Act to establish Albanian Heritage Month

## Summary

This enactment designates the month of November as “Albanian Heritage Month”.

## Short Title

### Section 1
*Short title*

This Act may be cited as the Albanian Heritage Month Act .

## Albanian Heritage Month

### Section 2
*Albanian Heritage Month*

Throughout Canada, in each year, the month of November is to be known as “Albanian Heritage Month”.
