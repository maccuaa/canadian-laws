# Bill C-213 — An Act to amend the Immigration and Refugee Protection Act (cessation of refugee protection)

## Summary

This enactment amends theto repeal provisions related to inadmissibility and loss of status resulting from the cessation of refugee protection for permanent residents. Immigration and Refugee Protection Act

## Immigration and Refugee Protection Act

### Section 1

Section 40.1 of the Immigration and Refugee Protection Act is repealed.

### Section 2

Paragraph 46(1)(c.1) of the Act is repealed.
