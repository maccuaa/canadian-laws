# Bill S-225 — National Thanadelthur Day Act

## Summary

This enactment designates the 5th day of February in each year as “National Thanadelthur Day”.

## Short Title

### Section 1
*Short title*

This Act may be cited as the .

## National Thanadelthur Day

### Section 2
*National Thanadelthur Day*

Throughout Canada, in each and every year, the 5th day of February is to be known as “National Thana­del­thur Day”.

### Section 3
*Not a legal holiday*

For greater certainty, National Thanadelthur Day is not a legal holiday or a non-juridical day.
