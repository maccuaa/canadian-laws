# Bill C-260 — An Act to amend the Criminal Code (medical assistance in dying — protection against coercion)

## Summary

This enactment amends theto make it an offence for certain persons in certain circumstances to initiate a discussion with a person about the availability to them of medical assistance in dying, knowing that they have not specifically requested to have such a discussion. Criminal Code

## Short Title

### Section 1
*Short title*

This Act may be cited as the Preventing Coercion of Persons Not Seeking Medical Assistance in Dying Act .
