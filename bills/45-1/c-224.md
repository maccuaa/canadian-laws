# Bill C-224 — An Act to amend the Food and Drugs Act (natural health products)

## Summary

This enactment amends theto provide that natural health products are not therapeutic products within the meaning of that Act and, therefore, are not subject to the same monitoring regime as other drugs. Food and Drugs Act

## Food and Drugs Act

### Section 1

The definition **therapeutic product** in section 2 of the Food and Drugs Act is replaced by the following:

### Section 2

Section 21.321 of the Act is repealed.

### Section 3

Subsection 21.8(2) of the Act is repealed.

## Transitional Provision

### Section 4
*Proceedings*

No proceedings may be commenced or continued in respect of an offence under section 31.2 or 31.4 of the Food and Drugs Act in relation to a product that is a within the meaning of the Natural Health Products Regulations if the offence was committed on or after the day on which section 500 of the Budget Implementation Act, 2023, No. 1 came into force and before the day on which this Act comes into force.
