# Bill C-264 — An Act to repeal certain restrictions on shipping

## Summary

This enactment repeals the. Oil Tanker Moratorium Act

## Oil Tanker Moratorium Act

### Section 1
*Repeal*

The Oil Tanker Moratorium Act , chapter 26 of the Statutes of Canada, 2019, is repealed.
