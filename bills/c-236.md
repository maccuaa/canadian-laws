# Bill C-236 — An Act to amend the Criminal Code, the Corrections and Conditional Release Act and the Prisons and Reformatories Act

## Summary

This enactment amends theto add as an aggravating factor for sentencing purposes and as a reason to delay parole the fact that a person who is convicted of certain offences refuses to provide persons in authority with information respecting the location of bodies or remains. It also amends theand theto add that fact as a consideration in the making of certain decisions under those Acts.
